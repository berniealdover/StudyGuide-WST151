Questions

What events and technologies helped the internet grow in the early 90s?

A convergance of technologies. User friendly operating systems and online service providers. Restrictions were lifted in 1991 and the Internet grew.

What is the purpose of organizations like W3C and IETF?

The Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF) is the principal body engaged in the development of new Internet protocol standard specifications.The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)Takes a proactive role in developing recommendations and prototype technologies related to the Web.

Why should you put extra effort into making websites more accessible?

It's the right thing to do and in some cases it's the law. It also helps with SEO and ranking.

What are 3 ways to make websites more accessible?

Don't use images for anything that can be displayed with text.
Add text descriptions for images.
Use descriptive links Provide title attributes in links.
Provide a text navigation area at the bottom of the page.
Use proper semantic html tags that introduce meaning rather than only presentation.
Declare the language.
Avoid dashes.
Avoid Abbreviation.
Avoid slang words.
Keep sentences short.

What is intellectual property?

Intellectual property (IP) refers to creations of the mind, such as inventions; literary and artistic works; designs; and symbols, names and images used in commerce.

When is it ok to use images, audio, or video, created by someone else?

If they creator provided a creative commons license or if the work is in the public domain.
